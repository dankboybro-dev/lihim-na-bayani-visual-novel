Lihim na Bayani is an educational visual novel created to bring the stories of Philippine Revolution figures to life. This interactive experience was designed for students to explore history in a more engaging and meaningful way.

Characters featured include: José Rizal, Andrés Bonifacio, Emilio Aguinaldo, Apolinario Mabini, Marcelo H. del Pilar, Graciano López Jaena, Antonio Luna, Mariano Ponce, and Gregorio del Pilar.
