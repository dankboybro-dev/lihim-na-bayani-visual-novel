About This Project

Lihim na Bayani is an educational visual novel created to bring the stories of the Philippine Revolution to life. This interactive experience was designed for students to explore history in a more engaging and meaningful way.

Historical Figures Featured

  • Andrés Bonifacio – Supremo of the Katipunan
  • Emilio Aguinaldo – President of the First Philippine Republic
  • José Rizal – National Hero, martyr
  • Gregoria de Jesús – Lakambini of the Katipunan
  • Apolinario Mabini – Brains of the Revolution
  • Antonio Luna – General, fiery patriot
  • Teresa Magbanua – Visayan commander
  • Mariano Álvarez – Revolutionary leader

Development

Created with HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript.

Educational purpose: Philippine History, Grade 8-12
